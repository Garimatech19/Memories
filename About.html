Here our journey starts....
College Life is one of the most remarkable and lovable times in an individual's life. Since we had completed a semester online , after commencement of offline classes we were impatiently waiting for the first day to come when we could be able to attend the college and live together. Even though it was just 3 months , I think we all have gotten very close and coincidentally we even became flatmates !! . We still remember the first day when we met , even though it was our first time it felt like we were well acquainted friends . it wasn't awkward at all . The first day of college will always be nerve-racking our mind will be filled with thoughts of the next morning and how things will turn out to be on the first day of college, how will your dress look on you, thoughts like these will be constantly running through our mind . But it was a bit different in our case . It was the same familiar faces so it was less stressful . In our college life, along we enjoyed a lot of other things. We traveled to lots of places, had new experiences, and learned many new things . We made new friends and created good memories which are yet to come further . We still are exploring things , finding our interests , domains that we enjoy and want to pursue . For example this website , even though we have not much knowledge about how exactly a website is done overall it was a fun process , informative and a new experience . All together , we got adjusted to each other and environment relatively better we are really having fun being together and able learning together .
